Azure Free Account Setup

Create and configure your Azure free account with $200 credit and access to popular cloud services

Project Overview

The Azure free account is your gateway to exploring Microsoft's cloud platform. It provides:

  • $200 credit to spend in the first 30 days
  • 12 months of free popular services
  • 25+ always-free products
  • Access to all Azure regions worldwide

Implementation Steps

1

Navigate to Azure Free Account

Visit the Azure Free Account page and click "Start free".

# Official Azure Free Account URL
https://azure.microsoft.com/free
2

Sign In with Microsoft Account

Use your existing Microsoft account or create a new one. You'll need:

  • Valid email address
  • Phone number for verification
  • Credit card for identity verification (no charges unless you upgrade)
3

Complete Identity Verification

Azure will verify your identity through:

  • Phone number verification via text or call
  • Credit card verification (a temporary $1 hold may appear)
  • Agreement to subscription terms
4

Explore Azure Portal

Once your account is created, you'll be redirected to the Azure Portal:

  • Familiarize yourself with the dashboard
  • Explore available services
  • Check your subscription and credit status
# Azure Portal URL
https://portal.azure.com
5

Configure Account Settings

Set up important account configurations:

  • Billing alerts: Set up spending limits
  • Resource groups: Organize your resources
  • Access control: Understand RBAC permissions
  • Service limits: Review free tier limitations

Best Practices

Account Security

  • Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Regularly review sign-in activity

Cost Management

  • Set up billing alerts at 50%, 75%, 90%, and 100% of credit
  • Use Azure Pricing Calculator before deploying resources
  • Clean up resources when not in use

Resource Organization

  • Use meaningful resource group names
  • Apply consistent tagging strategy
  • Follow naming conventions from the start
